About Us

At Ayrum, we bring over 20 years of hands-on experience delivering high-quality software and IT solutions to clients across industries. Proudly based in Ontario, Canada, we are founded by seasoned professionals with a mission to combine the reliability of enterprise-grade development with the agility and personal touch of a startup.
