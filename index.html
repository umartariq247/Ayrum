About Us

At Ayrum, we may be a new name in the market, but we bring over 15 years of hands-on experience delivering high-quality software and IT solutions to clients across industries.

We specialize in custom web applications, cloud solutions, Microsoft 365 & SharePoint services, and full-stack development that empower businesses to scale and thrive in a digital-first world.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality, transparency, and client success. Every project we take on is approached with the mindset of a long-term technology partner—not just a vendor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you're curious about our services, pricing, or how we work with clients across the globe, this FAQ section covers the most common questions we receive. We're here to make your IT journey simple, transparent, and affordable.

Who are you, and where are you based?

We are a Canadian-based IT consulting company specializing in custom software development, cloud solutions, and Microsoft technologies including SharePoint, Power Platform, and .NET. Our team is committed to delivering secure, scalable, and innovative digital solutions for businesses across industries.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of IT services including full-stack web development, mobile app development, cloud integration (Azure, AWS), SharePoint and Microsoft 365 solutions, IT strategy consulting, and custom application development.

Do you offer affordable website development for small businesses or startups?

Yes! We believe every business deserves a professional online presence—no matter the size or budget. Our simple website packages start at just $200 CAD, perfect for startups, personal portfolios, and small businesses looking for clean, responsive websites. We also offer flexible upgrades as your business grows.

Do you work with small businesses or only large enterprises?

We work with clients of all sizes—from startups and small businesses to large enterprises—providing tailored solutions that fit their unique needs and budgets.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

We follow industry best practices and use secure development lifecycle (SDL) approaches. We also ensure compliance with privacy and security standards such as GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO, depending on the client's requirements.

Can you work with clients outside of Canada?

Yes. While we’re based in Canada, we serve clients globally through virtual collaboration, secure communication channels, and cloud-based project management tools.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in Microsoft technologies such as .NET Core, C#, Azure, SharePoint, Power Apps, Power Automate, and SQL Server. We also work with modern front-end frameworks like React and Angular, and mobile platforms like Flutter and Xamarin.

How can I get started with your services?

Simply contact us through our website’s contact form or email us directly. We’ll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs and how we can help.
